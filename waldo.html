Where's Waldo?

Open Ended Project 3 - Hailey Schoppe - CS2300

Have you ever played Where's Waldo and been convinced that maybe, just this once, the author had forgot to draw Waldo in?

Well look no further! This project will search an image for you with opencv2 and tell you whether or not Waldo is present.
